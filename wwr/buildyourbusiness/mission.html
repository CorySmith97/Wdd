BuildYourBusiness's mission is to empower the small business owner. Many people wish to start their own business and take control of their lives. However a rude awakening comes to many. It is hard to manage a business. We provide a streamline set of tools to manage your business operations on a day to day scale. You have a small team? No problem! We help you make sure all members of your team have access to their individual calendars. Managin a business doesn't need to be a big scary thing. Let us help you out!

We aim to target small businesses in many manual labor fields such as roofing, plumbing, or other service based comapnies. BuildYourBusiness provides the tools necessary to grow your business without growing your stress levels. The idea for BYB came while I was working for a supervisor at a small roofing company. I couldn't help but notice the communication difficulties we had with the team. We had a calender that supervisors had access to, but not the crew members could see. I saw that if we could make a better system for each person in the company to understand their role, there would be much less confusion and everyone would be happier.

As a higher up I also saw the pain that managing invoices could be. It would be so nice if the invoices could easily be integrated into the calender so as I manager I could have access to all the important information related to the client. BYB was built in part with this in mind. Ease of sending and managing invoices to take the stress off you, the owner.
