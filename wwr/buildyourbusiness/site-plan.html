Overview

Purpose

The goal of SBB is to help small business manage all of their information in one centralized location. This website is a hub where invoices can be made, and sent to clients. Additionally, calender information can be planned here. With all of this we hope to help small business grow in a much less stressful way.

Audience

The audience is small business owners. Specifically small business in some form of manuel labor. This is the field that I have familiarity with, and would best be able to tailor the website to these owners.

Content

Home page

No business owner wants to spend all of their time working paperwork and making sure their calender is lined up. BuildYourBusiness is a web application with the purpose of helping small businesses streamline their day to day. The application offers many features to allow your to focus on doing the best job you can. We offer the a combination application that manages your calendar for each member of your team, as well as invoice and money management for your day to day operations. New Paragraph: Calendars are often times the main organizational tool that business owners use on a daily basis. We have taken notice of that and seek to streamline the calendar system that you use. Like many other competetors we offer the ability to manage multiple calendars so that each member of your team can see their daily tasks, and plan accordingly. In our experience as teams begin to utilize these tools, their businesses begin to grow, and the owner roll slowly is able to take a less stressed role. While it may seem simple, when the proper tools are available, works of art are able to be created. Please check out our mission to learn more about where BuildYourBusiness was first conceived! We look forward to be able to serve you and help you Build your business!

Images for the Home page

Our Mission

BuildYourBusiness's mission is to empower the small business owner. Many people wish to start their own business and take control of their lives. However a rude awakening comes to many. It is hard to manage a business. We provide a streamline set of tools to manage your business operations on a day to day scale. You have a small team? No problem! We help you make sure all members of your team have access to their individual calendars. Managin a business doesn't need to be a big scary thing. Let us help you out! New Paragrpah: We aim to target small businesses in many manual labor fields such as roofing, plumbing, or other service based comapnies. BuildYourBusiness provides the tools necessary to grow your business without growing your stress levels. The idea for BYB came while I was working for a supervisor at a small roofing company. I couldn't help but notice the communication difficulties we had with the team. We had a calender that supervisors had access to, but not the crew members could see. I saw that if we could make a better system for each person in the company to understand their role, there would be much less confusion and everyone would be happier. As a higher up I also saw the pain that managing invoices could be. It would be so nice if the invoices could easily be integrated into the calender so as I manager I could have access to all the important information related to the client. BYB was built in part with this in mind. Ease of sending and managing invoices to take the stress off you, the owner.

Pricing

No one wants to spend a lot of money for services that can be found for free in many places. We understand that. Our goal was to be approachable for the business that is just starting out, and have a decent pricing for scalability. That is where our entire pricing mentality comes from. Making it easy for you. PRICING: To start a new account it comes in at $20 a month for the first user. Each additional user afterwards comes in at an additional $5 a month until you reach 10 members. For each user after the first 10 the price is $3 a month. These prices all increase and all come from the same business account.
